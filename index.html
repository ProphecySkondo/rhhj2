Jeffrey Epstein

January 20, 1953 – August 10, 2019

Jeffrey Edward Epstein was an American financier and convicted sex offender who operated a decades-long trafficking network targeting underage girls. He was arrested in July 2019 on federal charges of sex trafficking and conspiracy.

Epstein was found dead in his cell at the Metropolitan Correctional Center on August 10, 2019 — ruled a suicide by the NYC medical examiner, though widely disputed. Beginning in 2024, a federal judge ordered thousands of pages of civil litigation documents unsealed, making them publicly available for the first time.
